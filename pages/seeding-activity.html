FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Seeding?

Seed movies and TV shows and enjoy rewards. Seeding increases download speeds and reduces buffering, enabling a smoother viewing experience for others to enjoy.

How are rewards calculated?

To prioritize early seeders, we'll use a decreasing exponential reward model:

Rewardn = R0 × e-k×(n-1)

n: Seeder's position (1 for the first seeder, 2 for the second, etc., up to 20).
R₀: Base reward for the first seeder per title.
k: Decay constant (higher k = steeper drop in rewards for later seeders).

Example Calculation for One Title

Assume R₀ = $0.20 and k=0.25:

  • First seeder: 0.20 × e-0.25×(1-1) = $0.20
  • Second seeder: 0.20 × e-0.25×(2-1) = $0.155
  • Third seeder: 0.20 × e-0.25×(3-1) = $0.121
  • ... up to 20 seeders.

Total reward per title ≈ $1.50.

Total for 5,000 titles = 5,000 × $1.50 = $7,500 / month.

Do I need a subscription to earn rewards?

No, you don't need to pay for a monthly subscription to earn Seeding rewards, but you do need to have a Telegram account to track rewards.

How do I redeem crypto rewards and withdraw them to my wallet?

Select the 'Claim' button to credit rewards to your account. Select the 'Withdraw' button to credit the rewards to your Telegram Wallet. Your Telegram Wallet will be used as your default wallet.

If you are connected to Streamflix via a different crypto wallet, it'll be credited to that wallet instead.
